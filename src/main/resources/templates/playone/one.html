Wild Beest Migration Safari

The Tanzania Migration Safari offers a once-in-a-lifetime chance to witness the Great Migration, where millions of wildebeests, zebras, and other herbivores move between Tanzania's Serengeti National Park and Kenya's Maasai Mara Reserve in search of fresh grazing.
