Wild Beest Migration Safari

The Tanzania Migration Safari offers a once-in-a-lifetime chance to witness the Great Migration, where millions of wildebeests, zebras, and other herbivores move between Tanzania's Serengeti National Park and Kenya's Maasai Mara Reserve in search of fresh grazing.

Key Facts & Panda's Information

Sample Itinerary:

Day 1–2: Arrive at Arusha/Kilimanjaro, rest or explore.

Day 3–6: Serengeti – game drives, river crossings, predator-prey sightings.

Day 7–10: Northern Serengeti & Mara River – watch dramatic crossings, enjoy walks and night drives.

Day 11–13: Central Serengeti – more wildlife and diverse landscapes.

Day 14: Depart via Arusha/Kilimanjaro.

Tips for success.

Travel during peak migration months (July–October).

Pack for varying weather, bring binoculars and camera.

Expect long game drives, stay patient.

Respect wildlife and follow guides.

Choose well-located camps or lodges for best views.

Best Time to Visit

Anytime, with Panda especially July to October, during the Mara River crossings
